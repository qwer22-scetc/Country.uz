Uzbekistan

Uzbekistan, officially the Republic of Uzbekistan, is a landlocked country in Central Asia. It is bordered by Kazakhstan to the northwest, Kyrgyzstan to the northeast, Tajikistan to the southeast, and Afghanistan to the south. It shares borders with Turkmenistan to the southwest and has a coastline along the Aral Sea to the northwest. Uzbekistan is the most populous country in Central Asia. Its capital and largest city is Tashkent.
